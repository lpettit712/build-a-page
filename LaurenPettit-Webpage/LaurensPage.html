Phase 1: Sugar Detox

Start off your challenge with a 1 week slim down plan. This plan with kick start weight loss. It will also help kick any bad eating habits. Each day in your slim down phase should follow the guide below. Work out 5 to 6 days a week. Work outs should include at least 30 minutes of cardio and 30-45 minutes of strength training. No soda (including diet) or articial sweetener of any kind. Stevia is the only sweetener allowed.

Meal No. Meal
1 Half cup egg whites with unlimited veggies. Coffee okay.
2 Grapefruit, Orange, Apple or Berries
3 4-6 oz of lean meat (Chicken, tofu, fish or ground turkey). Salad consisting of spinach and or kale. Top salad with any raw veggies.
4 Protein shake.
5 4-6 oz of lean meat (Chicken, tofu, fish or ground turkey) with green veggies.
6 Half a cup of greek yogurt with berries and/or cinnamon.

Phase 2: Establish Healthy Eating Habits

During this phase we can expand food selections to include foods such as quinoa and other vegetable carbs; potatos, yams, etc. Continue working out 5 to 6 days a week.

Meal No. Meal
1 Half cup egg whites with unlimited veggies with 1/4 cup of berries. Coffee okay.
2 Grapefruit, Orange, Apple or Berries
3 5oz of lean meat (Chicken, tofu, fish or ground turkey) with 1/4 cup of quinoa. Salad consisting of spinach and or kale. Top salad with any raw veggies.
4 Protein shake.
5 5oz of lean meat (Chicken, tofu, fish or ground turkey) with green veggies and 1/2 potato or yam.
6 Half a cup of greek yogurt with berries and/or cinnamon.

Phase 3: Maintain

Stick to the toning up standards for about 90% of your meals. Whole wheat pasta or brown rice dishes are fine to have a few times a week.
